✦ Our Story

A Culinary Legend
of Palakollu

Nestled in Venkateswara Colony, Aahvanam hotel has been the definitive address for authentic Hyderabadi cuisine. What began as a humble eatery has grown into one of the region's most beloved culinary institutions — a place where every dish carries the weight of tradition.

From our signature dum biryani slow-cooked with aromatic spices, to our legendary kebabs and rich curries — every plate tells the story of royal culinary heritage.
